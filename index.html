Barbara Whiting

Junior Full-stack Web Development

I have always had a passion for art and design since I was in grade school. I explored the many facets of art over the years, as an art student, an architecture student and in my freetime taking various art classes. So it was not a far stretch that I would fall in love with software development. To me web and software development are a form of art, through expressing myself through design, through my code, and in exploring the various methods that can be used.

Since early 2019 I have been exploring software development, starting with learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript on Codecademy. This got me curious and I knew that if I was serious about learning more that I needed a fully immersive experience, so I enrolled in the University of Denver’s Coding Bootcamp. A 26-week program that went well beyond what I learned in Codecademy, teaching me both front-end and back-end development.

After the bootcamp I decided to work freelance doing web development and learning new skills. I became a Certified ServiceNow Administrator and went back to school to get a Bachelor’s in Software Development. I have continued to teach myself ServiceNow working on ServiceNow Developer skills, working on personal projects all while studying full-time. I am extremely passionate about learning and am currently looking for an employer that is passionate about assisting me in continuing this journey.
